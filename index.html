About me

Let's face it, I am about as average as it gets. Red-hair, usually sunburnt, I still eat PB&Js for lunch, and I rode the bench throughout most of my sports career. I have tried to pimp out my about me, but fall to the same realization every time. I am average because I have not played the cards I was dealt, so this is the launchpad for me to become above average. Follow me on this journey to be above average and who knows, maybe we both will learn a thing or two along the way.
